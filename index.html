"We broke up 4 months ago after a fight. I want to reach out — not pressure her, just see how she's doing."

Hey — I know it's been a while and I've gone back and forth on whether to text. I'm not trying to reopen anything. I just realized I genuinely miss talking to you. Hope things have been good on your end.

Business · Cover Letter
"Applying for a Senior Product Designer role at Stripe. 6 years in fintech UX, led a team of 4."
Output · Version 1
Six years ago, I joined a payments startup with a checkout flow that took 11 steps to complete. When I left, it took three. That obsession with reducing friction is what draws me to Stripe — and to this role.

"Best man speech for my brother's wedding. Known him 28 years, he's terrible with directions but amazing with people."
Output · Version 1
In 28 years, I have watched my brother get lost in three airports, two car parks, and once — inexplicably — his own street. But I have never once seen him lose track of a person who matters to him.
